Having saluted the Supreme Diety (Narayana) and the highest of all male beings (Nara) and also Sarasvati, let there be a cry success!
नारायणं, नरोत्तमं नरं, सरस्वती-देवीं च नमस्कृत्य "जयम्" इत्यस्य घोषणा भवेत्!
Kulapati Shaunaka conducted a Yajna for 12 years.
कुलपतिः शौनकः द्वादशवर्षाणि यावत् यज्ञस्य निर्वहणं कृतवान्।
Sages were present for the Yajana.
यज्ञार्थम् उपस्थिताः आसन् मुनयः।
They were sitting comfortably in the Naimisha forest.
नैमिषारण्ये सौख्येन उपविष्टाः ते।
Ugrashrava was the son of Rishi Lomaharshana and knew the puranas.
ऋषेः लोमहर्षणस्य पुत्रः उग्रश्रवाः पुराणस्य ज्ञाता आसीत्।
He was also the son of a suta.
सः सूत-पुत्रः अपि।
He politely approached the Munis.
सः सविनयम् मुनीणां समीपे गतवान्।
Those sages wanted to listen to the Puranas from Ugrashravas.
ते मुनयः उग्रश्रवसः पुराण-कथाः श्रोतुम् ऐच्छन्।
The sages welcomed Ugrashravas.
मुनयः उग्रश्रवसः स्वागतं कृतवान्।
Ugrashravas greeted with folded hands.
अञ्जलिना अभिवादनं कृतवान् उग्रश्रवाः।
The munis offered a seat for Ugrashravas.
मुनयः आसनं प्रदत्तवान् उग्रश्रवसे।
The munis saw that Ugrashravas rested after sitting comfortable.
उग्रश्रवाः सौख्येन उपविश्य विश्रामं कृतवान् इति मुनयः दृष्टवन्तः।
One among the munis asked -
मुनीषु कश्चन पृष्टवान् यत् -
"Where have you come from, Ugrashravas?
"कुतः आगतवान् उग्रश्रवः? सर्वं वृत्तान्तं कथय।
"Where have you come from, Ugrashravas?
"कुतः आगतवान् उग्रश्रवः?
Where have you spent your time?

Ugrashravas replied -
उग्रश्रवाः उत्तरं दत्तवान् -
Ugrashravas replied -
उग्रश्रवाः उत्तरं दत्तवान् -
"I spend time at King Janamejaya's snake sacrifice.
"राज्ञः जनमेजयस्य सर्प-सत्रे समयः यापितः मया।
There I heard the Mahabharata composed by Krishna Dvaipayana who was also Veda Vyasa.
तत्र वेदव्यासेन कृष्ण-द्वैपायने रचिता महाभारत-कथा श्रुतवान् अहम्।
There I heard the Mahabharata composed by Krishna Dvaipayana who was also Veda Vyasa.
तत्र वेदव्यासेन कृष्ण-द्वैपायने रचिता महाभारत-कथा श्रुतवान् अहम्।
Retold by Vaishampayana.
वैशम्पायनेन श्राविता।
I visited many holy places.
बहूनां तीर्थक्षेत्राणां दर्शनं लब्धं मया।
Even Kurukshetra where the Mahbharata war took place.
कुरुक्षेत्रमपि गतवान् अहम् यत्र युद्धम् अभवत्।
All of you are like Lord Brahma himself. I came here yo see you all.
भवन्तः सर्वे ब्रह्मणे सदृशाः। भवतां दर्शनार्थम् अत्र आगतवान् अहम्।
To me all of you are like Lord Brahma himself.

मुनयः उक्तवान् यत् -
The munis said -
"The Mahabharata composed by Vyaasa and celebrated by the Gods and Brahmarshis.
"वेद-व्यासेन रचितं महाभारतं देवैः ब्रह्मर्षिभिः अभिपूजितम्।
Who is both non-existing and existing, who is the (existing) universe and still distinct from both the existing and the non-existing world, who is the originator of all, both high and low, who is ancient, great and undecaying.
असत् च सत् च एव च यत् विश्वम् सत् असतः परम्, परावराणां स्रष्टारं पुराणं परम् अव्ययम्।
Vishnu, one, who is sinless, who the guide immovable. who is worthy is Hari, of all pleasing and auspicious of all worship, pure and the lord of the faculties, the movable and the immovable.
हृषीकेशं चर-अचर-गुरुम् हरिम् मङ्गल्यं मङ्गलं विष्णुं वरेण्यम् अनघं शुचिम् नमस्कृत्य।
Vishnu, one, who is sinless, who the guide immovable. who is worthy is Hari, of all pleasing and auspicious of all worship, pure and the lord of the faculties, the movable and the immovable.

Some bards have already sung this history; and some again are teaching it to others; others will no doubt do the same hereafter on earth.

It is a great—source of knowledge all through the three¯worlds. It is possessed by the twice-born in detailed and compendious forms.
